PRODUCT EVIDENCE

Two bounded product surfaces.

App and Console interface surfaces are shown in separate controlled local captures with deterministic synthetic state. They are independent fixtures, not live telemetry, deployment proof, or a correlated customer run.

Auxtho App · independent synthetic fixture · controlled local capture · 16 Jul 2026

Governed review states rendered in the App

A controlled local rendering of the App Overview component shows a coherent 12-packet fixture: four need review, two are blocked, three are ready for release, one was released today, and two remain in progress.

Fixture boundary: controlled local component rendering, fixed responses, no customer data, and no external network requests. The capture does not establish source revision, deployment identity, live use, backend aggregation, or completed customer releases.

Auxtho Console · independent synthetic fixture · controlled local capture · 16 Jul 2026

Operator attention rendered in the Console

A controlled local rendering of the Console metric component shows four review-attention events, two critical signals, three invented customer records, and one invented active bootstrap from a fixed fixture.

Fixture boundary: controlled local component rendering, invented records, no customer data, no application or external API call, no provider or export path, and no production mutation. The capture does not establish source revision or deployment identity and is not the continuation of the App fixture.

Evidence provenance and limits are recorded in the public evidence manifest.

SELECTED REVIEW PATH

Exception signal
